About

I spent over a decade at KPMG working inside major companies in oil and gas, banking, insurance, pharmaceuticals, consumer goods, and fintech. My job was to understand how complex businesses actually run and help them reduce risk, improve margins, and prepare for public markets.

At Amazon, I run financial operations for inbound fulfillment across the East region – a mix of operations, strategic finance, and AI-driven process improvements.

I take what I’ve learned in large, complex environments and translate it into simple, durable systems for real businesses — so they make more money, solve recurring issues, and make life easier for the people carrying the load. I want the work I do with people to actually carry weight—in their day‑to‑day life and in the way they’re seen by the people who depend on them.

I’m also using my page to test new tools, workflows, and AI-driven systems, in case you come across anything surprising, unusual, or that feels a bit…different…
